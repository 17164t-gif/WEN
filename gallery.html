File 03 / 35mm film

Film
gallery

This page is only for film photographs. I use a Leica M6 with 400-speed film because film does not just record a place; it records the weather, the waiting, the walking and the small hesitation before pressing the shutter.

For me, the meaning of travel is not only arriving somewhere famous. It is noticing what the road gives back: light on a gate, people crossing a square, a beach sign, a row of tulips, the exact blue of the sea. These frames are arranged by place, three images per row, like contact sheets from different chapters of the same trip.

Rome / Italy

Rome

Rome always feels larger than the camera, so I like photographing it in pieces: stone, stairs, reflections, crowds and light.

Istanbul / Turkey

Istanbul

Istanbul is movement: water, taxis, flags, markets, ferries and the city changing color between two shores.

Amalfi / Italy

Amalfi

Amalfi is where the roll becomes bright: sea, beach, cafes and cliff towns that almost overexpose into memory.

Turin / Italy

Turin

Turin is quieter, more architectural, and the 400 film keeps its shadows soft without making the city too perfect.
