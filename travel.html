Polaroid
travel archive

Each city opens into its own Polaroid page. The entrances stay uniform, like a small box of instant photographs arranged on one table.

I like that Polaroids never repeat themselves. Even if I stand in the same place twice, the second image will have a different color, a different shadow, maybe even a small accident at the edge. That uncertainty makes travel feel alive.
