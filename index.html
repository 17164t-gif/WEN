WEN ZIYI
Visual Diary

A multi-page photo journal for instant film, 35mm travel works and relaxed daily fragments.

I keep coming back to old cameras because they make time feel physical. Digital cameras are precise and convenient, but Polaroid has another rhythm: press the shutter, wait for the image to appear, and accept whatever little accident the light has left for you. Every frame is a tiny blind box, and that uncertainty is part of the joy.
